ViT vs CNN Models for OCT Classification

A study of deep leanring models for medical imaging classification task. This aim of this project study was to compare the CNN and Vision-Transformer models while using transfer-learning.Experiments suggest that ViT can give better results than CNN when trained on large-datasets or using transfer-learning. Github

Robustness Analysis of Image Segmentation Models

A comparative study of DeepLabV3+ and Segformer against image perturbations. We analysed the performance of DeepLabV3+ and Segformer under Image-cropping, patch-shuffling and domain shift. Experiments show that Segformer is more robust to patch level changes than DeepLabV3+ due to its global feature extraction scheme. Github

Age-Related Effective Brain Connectivity Analysis

Causal connectivity analysis among language comprehension associated brain regions in different age groups. The objective of this study was to find brain regions responsible for language comprehension degradation and compensation.
